Cold Tofu

Description

Cold tofu is a very refreshing food that's perfect for summer days! It is very quick to prepare and only uses a few ingredients that you likely have laying around in your pantry.

This dish can be made with any tofu texture, but silken tofu is recommended.

Ingredients

  • Silken Tofu
  • Spring Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Water
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, toast 1 tsp of sesame seeds on low heat until fragrant.
  2. Add 3 tbsp of mirin and simmer until all of the alcohol evaporates.
  3. Add 3 tbsp of spy sauce (can be subsituted with tamari).
  4. Let simmer until sauce thickens, add water to taste.
  5. Pour sauce over silken tofu. Garnish with spring onions and add a drizzle of sesame oil.
